## The strategic insight to carry forward

Every loop proved the model reproduces whatever floor it is given: it
composes and wires well, but cannot invent engine-feel (per-pixel physics,
SFX timing, animation) inside a bounded budget. Prompting will not close a
100× gap; **deterministic C engineering will**. The next step is a shared
game-feel library in the starter project, with skeletons rebuilt on it, so
the model's job becomes composition. Details and loop breakdown (G1a/G1b):
`docs/2026-07-17-p2-plan.md`.
